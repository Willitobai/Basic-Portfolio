William Baires originally from El Salvador is a Bay Area photographer/artist. He has a natural eye for photo composition and exceptional attention to detail. He has an amazing ability to capture beautiful, genuine moments of family and friends. His contagious upbeat attitude and great sense of humor help his clients feel comfortable and at ease, which helps produce the most authentic, natural looking shots and portraits. Whether you are getting married, expecting a new baby, or simply looking to capture the love you feel for your friends and family on print, William is there to catch it all. When he's not behind the camera, you can find him painting in his shared studio space in San Francisco.
